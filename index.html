How to play

  • Press Spacebar to jump (click/tap also works).
  • Fly between pipes: +1 point for each pipe passed.
  • Pick up a basketball (you can carry only one).
  • When a hoop appears, fly through it while carrying a ball to dunk for +3 points.
  • If you hit a pipe, the ground, or the ceiling: game over.

Tip: The hoop “scores” when your player passes through its ring while carrying a ball.
