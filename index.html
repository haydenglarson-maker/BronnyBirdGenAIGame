Bronny Bird

Flap. Grab. Dunk. 💥

How to play

  • Press Space to jump between pipes.
  • Earn +1 point for every pipe you pass.
  • Pick up a basketball (carry only one).
  • Dunk it at a random hoop for +3 points (💥 explosion!).
  • Hit a pipe = game over.
